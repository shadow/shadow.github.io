Mailing Lists

General support is provided by sending email to shadow-support@cs.umn.edu. You may subscribe using the shadow-support mailman interface, or just browse the archives. Development questions or discussion can be had by emailing shadow-dev@cs.umn.edu. You may subscribe using the shadow-dev mailman interface, or just browse the archives.

Bug Reports

Shadow bugs can be reported on our github issue tracker.
